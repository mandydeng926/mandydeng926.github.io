About me

I’m Mengdie (Mandy), a Job Market Candidate for the academic position begins in Fall 2024. I shall obtain my doctoral degree at The University of Hong Kong, Accounting Department, with an anticipation date of June 2024.

As an accounting researcher, I am driven by an innate curiosity to explore how accounting processes in diverse business scenarios affect human decision-making. I have done researches in Capital Market Regulations, Disclosures, ESG, and Carbon Markets based on scientific methodologies and advanced technologies based on textual analysis and machine learning. One of my papers has been accepted by the Journal of Financial Markets.

I care about promoting open, collaborative, and diversified research and teaching environment. My academic trainings in HKU, coupled with my prior industry experience, have equipped me to be an effective and practical researcher and instructor.
